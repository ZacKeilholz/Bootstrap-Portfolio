About Me:

Hey There!

Hey There! My name is Zac, and I am a designer/diy-er/maker/tries-to-be-fun person who lives on the North side of Minneapolis, Minnesota. I have a dog named 'Yoni,' an amazing girlfriend, and too many project vehicles and motorcycles to fit in my single-car garage. After dropping out of a small liberal arts college in 2008 (majoring in Russian Studies and Mathematics), I moved to Minneapolis in pursuit of the big city life and to be just a number at the University of Minnesota- Twin Cities.

I dropped out yet again to pursue a job handling baggage, working the ticket counter, and more importantly, smuggling French Bulldog puppies under the radar of my employer both nationally and internationally for some mad cash profit. At some point, I decided I didn't like sleeping in airports, and I didn't get into an electrican apprenticeship in my dream city of Portland, OR, so I decided to get back into my studies (left off at academic probation) at the U of M- and ultimately graduated with honors in Architecture and Product Design.

To save money while finishing up my last two years in school, I gutted and converted a 1960's travel trailer into an almost-complete living space. I stealthily parked it in Northeast, Minneapolis where I successfully survived two Minnesota winters without running water. More recently, I have been a teaching assistant in the Product Design department at the U of M and have worked for two years in the architecture industry designing, engineering, and fabricating complex curving steel structures using a mixture of modeling in Rhinoceros 3d and Grasshopper scripting. My current goal is to be the very best- like no one ever was. To catch them is my real test, to train them is my cause. I will travel across the land searching far and wide. Teach Pokemon to understand the power that's inside.

Pokemon! Gotta catch 'em all!
